FOREST FIRE & FWI PREDICTION

Instructions
  1. This webapp uses ensemble technique for making final prediction about FWI or FIRE occurance.

  2. The top two models from same stategy have been ensembled togather and the models with different strategies will copuled togather to make final prediction. However this coupling will be dependent on the information provided.

  3. You can either provide information through form or can uplaoad CSV file in proper format.

  4. You can use this app for Fire prediction as well as FWI prediction

  5. For Fire prediction all field are mandatory but month is optional

  6. For FWI prediction you can either provide all information mentioned in the form or can skip the optional fields. The app will take care of that.

  7. File tha you have to upload must have columns wih headers 'month', 'Temperature','RH', 'Ws','Rain',FFMC', 'FWI',DMC, DC and ISI for Fire prediction and for FWI prediction month and FWI can be omitted.
  8. You can download the final result as csv or excel file
